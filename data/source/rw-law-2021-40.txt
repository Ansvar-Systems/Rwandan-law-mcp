LAW N° 040/2021 OF 28/07/2021
AMENDING LAW N° 005/2021
05/02/2021
GOVERNING
MUTUAL
LEGAL ASSISTANCE IN CRIMINAL
MATTERS
We, KAGAME Paul,
President of the Republic;
THE PARLIAMENT HAS ADOPTED AND
WE SANCTION, PROMULGATE THE
FOLLOWING LAW AND ORDER IT
PUBLISHED
IN
THE
OFFICIAL
GAZETTE OF THE REPUBLIC
RWANDA
THE PARLIAMENT:
The Chamber of Deputies, in its sitting of
July 2021;
Pursuant to the Constitution of the Republic
Rwanda of 2003 revised in 2015, especially
Articles 24, 29, 64, 69, 70, 88, 90, 91, 93, 106,
120, 122 and 176;
Having reviewed Law Nº 005/2021
05/02/2021 governing mutual legal assistance
in criminal matters;

ADOPTS:
Article One: Scope of this Law
Article 2 of Law nº 005/2021 of 05/02/2021
governing mutual legal assistance in criminal
matters is amended as follows:
“This Law applies to the request for legal
assistance in criminal matters to or from
foreign State in regards to the following:
1° identifying and locating persons;
2° taking evidence or statements from
persons;
3° effecting service of documents;
4° executing search and seizure;
5° providing and producing original
certified documents, records, items
other materials;

6° facilitating the appearance of a needed
witness in the requesting country;
7° effecting a temporary transfer
persons in custody to assist
investigation or for their appearance
witnesses;
8° tracing,
identifying,
freezing,
restraining and seizing proceeds
instrumentalities of crime;
9° returning and disposing of property;
10° preserving and obtaining computer data;
11° monitoring
postal
telecommunications;
12° any other lawful form of mutual
assistance or evidence gathering.
items
Without prejudice to other provisions of
Law, nothing in this Law prevents

competent authority from:
1° conducting an investigation on behalf
of a counterpart authority in a foreign
country;
2° having other forms of cooperation with
a counterpart authority in a foreign
country;
3° granting of any other form or nature
assistance that may lawfully
afforded to a counterpart authority
foreign country, including controlled
delivery, joint investigations, the use
other special investigative techniques
and the transfer of criminal
proceedings;
4° spontaneously transmitting information
relating to criminal matters to
competent authority in a foreign
country ;
5° concluding bilateral or multilateral
agreements or arrangements on
operation in criminal matters with
State, international organization
entity;

6° rendering of a broader range
assistance to another State than may
provided for in an agreement where
deemed necessary by the two parties
7° providing to any State, international
organization or entity assistance of
other form than is provided for under
this Law.
Article 2: Drafting, consideration
adoption of this Law
This Law was drafted in English, considered
and adopted in Ikinyarwanda.
Article 3: Repealing provision
All prior legal provisions contrary to this Law
are repealed.

Article 4: Commencement
This Law comes into force on the date of
publication in the Official Gazette of
Republic of Rwanda.
