THE PARLIAMENT:
The Chamber of Deputies, in its sitting of
October 2020;
Pursuant to the Constitution of the Republic
Rwanda of 2003 revised in 2015, especially
Articles 27, 30, 31, 32, 33, 64, 70, 88, 89, 90,
93, 106, 120, 122 and 176;

Having reviewed Law n° 017/2020 of 07/10/2020
establishing the general statute governing Public
servants;
ADOPTS:
Article one: Maternity leave
In Law n° 017/2020 of 07/10/2020 establishing
the general statute governing public servants,
Article 21 bis is inserted to read as follows:
“Article 21 bis: Maternity leave
A female public servant who gives birth has
right to maternity leave of twelve (12)
consecutive weeks including two (2) weeks
may take before delivery.
A female public servant who wishes to take
maternity leave submits to the director of human
resources a certificate of a recognized healthcare
entity with a copy thereof to her immediate
supervisor”.

Article 3: Commencement

of Rwanda. It takes effects as of 8 October 2020.
