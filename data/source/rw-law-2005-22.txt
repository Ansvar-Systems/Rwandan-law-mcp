YEMEJE:
Ingingo ya mbere:
Hashyizweho Pariki y’Igihugu ya Nyungwe.
Pariki y’Igihugu ya Nyungwe igizwe n’Icyanya cy’Ishyamba rikomye rya Nyungwe, gifite ubuso bungana na
hegitari ibihumbi ijana na kimwe na magana atanu na cumi n’eshanu na ari mirongo itanu n’icyenda (ha
101.515,59), Icyanya cy’Ishyamba rya Cyamudongo gifite ubuso bungana na hegitari magana ane na mirongo
itatu na ari mirongo itatu n’umunani (ha 430,38) n’Icyanya cy’ishyamba rya Gisakura gifite ubuso bungana na
hegitari cumi n’imwe na ari mirongo irindwi (ha 11,70). Byose hamwe bifite ubuso bungana na hegitari
ibihumbi ijana na kimwe na magana cyenda na mirongo itanu n’indwi na ari mirongo itandatu n’indwi (ha
101.957,67). Imbibi za Pariki y’Igihugu ya Nyungwe zigenwa n’iri tegeko ziri mu mugereka wa mbere waryo.
Ingingo ya 2:
Hashyizweho akarere k’ubuhumekero ka Pariki y’Igihugu ya Nyungwe gafite ubuso bungana na hegitari
ibihumbi icumi na mirongo inani n’eshanu na ari makumyabiri n’ebyiri (ha 10.085, 22). Imbibi z’akarere
k’ubuhumekero zishyirwaho n’iri tegeko ziri mu mugereka wa kabiri waryo.
Ingingo ya 3:
Imikoreshereze n’imicungire y’akarere k’ubuhumekero ikorwa hakurikijwe amategeko ariho agenga
imikoreshereze y’ubutaka, ibidukikije n’amashyamba.

Ingingo ya 5:

LAW AND ORDER IT TO BE PUBLISHED IN THE OFFICIAL GAZETTE OF THE REPUBLIC OF
RWANDA.
THE PARLIAMENT;
The Chamber of Deputies in its session of June 27, 2005;
Given the Constitution of the Republic of Rwanda of June 4, 2003 as amended to date, especially in its articles
49, 62, 90, 91, 92, 93, 108, 118, 121, 201 and 202;
Given the organic Law n° 04/2005 of April 8, 2005 determining the modalities of protection, conservation and
promotion of the environment in Rwanda;
Given the organic Law n° 08/ 2005 of July 14, 2005 determining the use and management of land in Rwanda;
Given the Law n° 32/2003 of September 6, 2003 modifying and complementing decree Law of April 26, 1974
approving and modifying Law of June 18, 1973 establishing the Office of Tourism and National Parks in
Rwanda;
Given the Law n° 47/1988 of December 5, 1988 organizing the forest regime;
Revisiting the Rwanda-Urundi Ordinance n° 83 bis/Agri of December 12, 1933 as modified by Rwanda-Urundi
Ordinance n° 33/Agri of May 24, 1934 establishing the reserved forest of Nyungwe;
ADOPTS:
Article one:
There is hereby established the Nyungwe National Park.
The Nyungwe National Park constitutes the Nyungwe National Reserve with an area of one hundred and one
thousand and one, five hundred and fifteen hectares and fifty nine acres (101,515. 59 ha), Cyamudongo Natural
Forest with an area of four hundred and thirty hectares and thirty eight acres (430.38 ha) and Gisakura Natural
Forest with an area of eleven hectares and seventy acres(11.70 ha). The total area is equivalent to one hundred and
one thousand and nine hundred fifty seven and sixty seven acres (101,957.67 ha) The boundaries of Nyungwe
National Park governed by this law are described in annex 1 .
Article 2:
There is hereby established a buffer zone of Nyungwe National Park with an area of ten thousand and eighty five
hectares and twenty two acres (10,085. 22 ha). The boundaries of the buffer zone determined by this law are
described in annex 2.
Article 3:
The use and anagement of the buffer zone are conducted in accordance with the laws in force, determining the use
of land, environment and forests.
Article 4:
All previous legal provisions contrary to this law are abrogated.

déterminées par la présente loi sont indiquées à l’annexe 1.

Il est créé une Zone Tampon du Parc National de Nyungwe dont la superficie est de dix mille quatre-vingt-cinq
hectares et vingt-deux ares (10.085,22 ha). Les limites de la zone tampon du Parc National de Nyungwe
déterminées par la présente loi sont indiquées à l’annexe 2.

forestier, foncier et environnemental en vigueur.
Article 4 :
